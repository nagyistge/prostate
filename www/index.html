Prostate Cancer Prediction

A tool for short and long term prediction of prostate cancer outcomes.

We have developed a model to predict short and long term prostate cancer re-occurrence using random survival forests with competing risks, using data from men with prostate cancer who are at risk of disease recurrence. This tools allows for prediction of patient information to generate predicted probabilities of disease recurrence and death.
